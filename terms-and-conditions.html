🏢 Part B — Placements
§ 05
Nature of Placement Service

The GradeWise Placements section is a curated job-opportunity board that serves two distinct streams of content:

  • Campus & Off-Campus Drives — Manually curated placement drives, walk-in interviews, and recruitment events sourced and posted directly by the GradeWise team.
  • Live Remote Jobs — Real-time job listings automatically aggregated from third-party public job APIs including Remotive, Arbeitnow, and Jobicy. These listings are refreshed automatically approximately every 5 minutes.
Important: GradeWise serves as an information aggregator for placements. It does not act as a recruiter, placement agency, or employment intermediary. All applications and employment relationships are formed solely between users and the respective employers.
§ 06
Campus & Off-Campus Drive Listings

Placement drives manually listed on this platform by the GradeWise team are intended to help students of relevant institutions discover recruitment events. Users should be aware of the following:

  • Campus Drives — These are drives typically conducted at a specific college or institution. Eligibility may be restricted to students of that institution. GradeWise does not guarantee admission to or participation in any campus drive.
  • Off-Campus Drives — These are drives open to candidates from multiple institutions. GradeWise provides listing information only; the selection process is managed entirely by the recruiting company.
  • Walk-In Interviews — These allow direct walk-in attendance without prior scheduling. GradeWise is not responsible for venue changes, cancellations, or alterations to walk-in drive details.
  • Deadlines — Application deadlines are displayed where available. GradeWise does not guarantee the accuracy of deadline information and users must verify these with the original source.
  • Package Information — CTC/LPA figures displayed are indicative and sourced from publicly available information. Actual compensation may vary and should be confirmed directly with the recruiting company.
Notice: GradeWise does not manage, influence, or participate in the selection, shortlisting, or offer process for any placement drive listed on this platform.
§ 07
Live Job Aggregation (Remotive · Arbeitnow · Jobicy)

The "Live Jobs" feature within the Placements section fetches and displays real-time job listings from the following third-party public job APIs:

  • Remotive — Specialises in remote tech and software jobs globally.
  • Arbeitnow — Aggregates job listings primarily in the European market, including remote roles.
  • Jobicy — A remote job board covering roles across software, data, design, and other domains.

These listings are classified by domain (Tech/Software, Data/AI-ML, Design/UI-UX) based on automated keyword analysis of job titles and tags. Domain classification is approximate and may not always accurately reflect the full scope of a role.

  • Live job listings are fetched directly from external APIs and are not verified, reviewed, or endorsed by GradeWise.
  • The auto-refresh cycle is approximately every 5 minutes; listings displayed may not represent the absolute latest state of availability on the source platform.
  • Duplicate listings may occasionally appear due to the same role being listed across multiple API sources. GradeWise applies deduplication logic but cannot guarantee its completeness.
  • GradeWise is not responsible for the accuracy, legality, or authenticity of any live job listing sourced from these APIs.
Disclaimer: GradeWise is not affiliated with Remotive, Arbeitnow, or Jobicy. These are independent third-party services and their listing data is used under their respective public API terms.

  • Final eligibility determination rests solely with the recruiting organisation, not with GradeWise.
Notice: Users must not rely solely on GradeWise's CGPA eligibility indicators when deciding whether to apply for a placement opportunity. Always verify eligibility criteria directly with the company or your institution's placement cell.
